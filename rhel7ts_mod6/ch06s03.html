6.3. Problem Replication

In this section you will learn to:
  • Replicate problem for confirmation and evaluation
A key to ensuring success at resolving problems is being able to replicate the problem at will. Being able to repeat the problem also gives us a greater understanding of the problem triggers.
As mentioned earlier, our observation skills and experience may allow us to see significant clues or symptoms that the reporter may have considered unimportant, insignificant, or unnoticeable. We can bring focused monitoring tools to the replication that can expose additional information that is not normally visible.
Lastly, if we cannot replicate the problem, how do we truly know that the problem has been fixed?
