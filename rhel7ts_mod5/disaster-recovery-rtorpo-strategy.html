5.14. Disaster Recovery RTO/RPO Strategy

Incomplete RTOs and RPOs can quickly derail a disaster recovery plan.
  • Every item in the DR plan requires a defined recovery point and time objective, as failure to create them may lead to significant problems that can extend the disaster’s impact.
  • Once the RTO and RPO metrics have been mapped to IT infrastructure, the DR planner can determine the most suitable recovery strategy for each system.
