5.5. Backup Challenges and Virtualization

New challenges exist in backing up virtual systems.
  • Being able to restore specific files on demand will never go away, even with virtualization.
  • Finding a way to avoid backing up data twice (entire virtual disk and filesystem level backups) to save space and I/O when running backups. This is known as data deduplication.
