5.15. Disaster Recovery Budget

The organization ultimately sets the IT budget and therefore the RTO and RPO metrics need to fit with the available budget.
While most business unit heads would like zero data loss and zero time loss, the cost associated with that level of protection may make the desired high availability solutions impractical.
A cost-benefit analysis often dictates which disaster recovery measures are implemented.
