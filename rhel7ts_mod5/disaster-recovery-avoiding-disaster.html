5.18. Disaster Recovery: Avoiding Disaster

In addition to preparing for the need to recover systems, organizations also implement precautionary measures with the objective of preventing a disaster in the first place.
These may include:
  • Local mirrors of systems and/or data and use of disk protection technology such as RAID
  • Surge protectors — to minimize the effect of power surges on delicate electronic equipment
  • Use of an uninterruptible power supply (UPS) and/or backup generator to keep systems going in the event of a power failure
  • Fire prevention/mitigation systems such as alarms and fire extinguishers
  • Strong physical security to limit access to hardware
  • Provide adequate heating/cooling even in the event of power outage
